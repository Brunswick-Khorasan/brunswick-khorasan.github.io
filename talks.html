An Introduction to Autostackable Groups

UNL GST Seminar, 2025-03-25

Autostackable groups, as first introduced by Susan Hermiller, Mark Brittenham, and Derek Holt, are a class of groups characterized by a prefix-closed convergent rewriting system. In this talk, I will introduce and show an example of this class of groups.
